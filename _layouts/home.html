I am a software developer, flashcards enjoyer, and gamer. Some stuff I've worked on:

  • Anki Books is a flashcards and note-taking app that creates Anki flashcard deck files to import into Anki. These .NET notes are a good example of what a book written with this app would look like. I also developed a different version of the app to learn about ASP.NET Core Blazor (many of the below blog posts are related to that).
  • Anki Record is a Ruby gem for creating and updating Anki flashcard deck files, which are zip files of SQLite databases. (It is how Anki Books makes Anki flashcard decks.)
  • Anki magnifying glass mouse cursor is an Anki add-on that some people have found useful.

My current focus is ASP.NET Core/React and solving Leetcode problems.
